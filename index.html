PCB Manufacturing for Every Application

Prototyping

Small run groups of less than 10 are supported for prototyping. We support placing unique components per board for prototyping and testing purposes. Any assemblies requiring 3D printing can be done in house.

Small to Medium PCBs

We can assemble PCBs up to 12" by 8" (304.8mm by 203.2mm). Customer supplied boards are supported, or we'll source boards for you based on your specifications.

Component Types

All through hole applications are supported. All SMD components are supported for 0402 components or larger. Continuity testing and inspection of each board is done after assembly. Customer provided components are supported, or we'll source components based on your specifications.

Assembly

Placement of assembled PCBs into enclosures, harnesses, or fixtures is supported. Enclosure parts in most cases must be provided, but some prototyping enclosures can be manufactured in house. Packaging for individual sale can also be provided. All assemblies past SMT/THT depend on the needs of customer, so feel free to contact us with any questions!

Medium Volume

We are set up to do medium volume production to get your product to market on time. Our maximum production rates will depend on the application, the design of the board, and any additional assemblies or packaging. If you have any questions about production volume or time please contact us!
